# Aeternum Map - Your New World companion

Don't get lost and use Aeternum Map to display your position on a pure map on second screen, overlay or as minimap in-game. 

## Features

- AGS ToS accepted
- Share live status with other applications or websites like [https://aeternum-map.th.gl](https://aeternum-map.th.gl)
- Live Tracking of your In-Game position
- Minimap
- Automatic detection of your In-Game character
- Auto start with New World
